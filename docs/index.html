Welcome

Welcome to Jaymeson Pon Music. I'm a young professional who has been involved with music since I was six years old. On this website you'll find some information about me and the music services I offer. Please contact me if you have questions about anything.

Artist Bio

I started private piano lessons at the age of six, learning through the Suzuki method. After six years of lessons, I transferred to a new teacher under whom I studied for another five years. I then enrolled at The University of Nebraska to study Piano Performance under Dr. James Johnson. I was 17-years old that first semester. I studied for 2 years in the music program and then turned my attention to Business, earning a Bachelor's of Science in Business Administration three years later. Though my day job is accounting, I stay busy with music. I currently play in Moon River Steamboat, an Omaha-based swing-dance band, give private piano lessons and write music for fun.

Moon River Steamboat

Moon River Steamboat is a four-person Omaha swing band that plays at local dance halls, bars and lounges. Recently formed in the late summer of 2018, it's current members include upright bassist Ilya, singer Kyla, drummer Jeff and keyboardist Jaymeson. All the band members knew one another from the music program they all attended at the University of Nebraska at Omaha in the late 2000's. Jaymeson and Ilya met up again by chance at a swing dance class hosted by a local nonprofit dance organization. After jamming a few times together in a small lesson studio, Ilya decided to officially form a band, inviting Jeff and, later Kyla, to join.

The band's set lists focus primarily on 30's and 40's music but does include some tracks from the later decades. They currently have no released albums.

Lessons

I teach students of all ages and skill-levels ranging from absolute beginner through intermediate. I also offer some theory and composition instruction for beginners. Usually, I incorporate theory into piano lessons, since every musician will benefit from a good understanding of musical concepts. Currently, I only teach private lessons in association with the 402 Arts Collective. I use their facilities and scheduling system.
